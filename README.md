# Shopify Growth Ebook

A comprehensive guide for D2C brands and Shopify store owners on how to set up, optimize, and grow a successful Shopify store.

**Start reading below** or jump to the [Table of Contents](TOC.md).

## Overview

This ebook is divided into two main sections:

1. **Technical Setup** – Store configuration, essential apps, and best practices
2. **Growth & Marketing** – Proven strategies to acquire, convert, and retain customers

## Target Audience

- New Shopify store owners
- Direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands
- Ecommerce entrepreneurs looking to scale
